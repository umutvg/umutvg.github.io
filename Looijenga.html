Mirror symmetry for Looijenga interiors and beyond

This is a hybrid conference that will take place on July 25-29, 2022. Its physical location will be IMBM, a math institute in Istanbul, located inside the beautiful campus of Bogazici University. The preceeding week there will be a summer school, mainly targeted towards local participants but open to everyone. Here is a poster.

The summer school will involve talks by a subset of the speakers and the organizers. The tentative plan for the lectures looks as follows (times and some of the speakers to be announced):

Day 1
- Introduction to mirror symmetry (2 Lectures, Bernd Siebert, Umut Varolgunes)
- Log CY varieties (Ozgur Kisisel)
- Discussion session

Day 2
- Classification of Looijenga interiors (2 lectures, Travis Mandel)
- Symplectic structures and Lagrangian fibrations on Looijenga interiors (Umut Varolgunes)
- Weinstein manifolds and their invariants (Dogancan Karabas)

Day 3
- Looijenga conjecture (Philip Engel)
- GHKS construction (Hulya Arguz)
- Keating-Hacking theorem (Umut Varolgunes)
- Discussion session

Day 4
- Examples (Ozgur Kisisel)
- Shende-Treumann-Williams construction I (Dogancan Karabas)
- Cluster varieties and Fock-Goncharov mirror symmetry (Pierrick Bousseau)
- Discussion session

Day 5
- Shende-Treumann-Williams construction II (Dogancan Karabas)
- Floer theory based mirror constructions (Yoel Groman)
- Intrinsic mirror symmetry (Hulya Arguz)
- Discussion session
