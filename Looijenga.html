Mirror symmetry for Looijenga interiors and beyond

This is hybrid conference that will take place on July 25-29, 2022. Its physical location will IMBM, a math institute in Istanbul, located inside the beautiful campus of Bogazici University. The preceeding week there will be a summer school, mainly targeted towards local participants. Here is a document that explains the mathematical goals of the event.
