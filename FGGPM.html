Feza Gürsey Mathematics and Physics Meetings

The goal of these 1-day events is to foster communication and collaboration between mathematicians and theoretical physicists. Each meeting will have an informal introductory component as well as a formal research talk. Meetings will take place on Friday's at Feza Gursey Center for Physics and Mathematics which is located in the Kandilli campus of Bogazici University. For transportation please check here.
